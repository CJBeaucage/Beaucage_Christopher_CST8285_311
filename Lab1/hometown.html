Windsor, Ontario

A small town south of Detroit, known for cars, pizza, and bars.

Last updated 2022/09/14

Windsor has a population of over 230,000 people. It historically was used as a hub for rum runners during prohibition. It is a very diverse city, where you can find every type of culture on a single street. Windsor is surrounded by farmland, and is a great supplier of Canadian Wine.

Image from City of Windsor, https://www.citywindsor.ca/visitors/pages/visiting-windsor.aspx

Here are some of the nice things to do in Windsor

  1. Get some Windsor pizza, the best pizza in Canada.
  2. Visit local distilleries and enjoy Canadian whiskey & rye.
  3. Visit local breweries and enjoy their beers.
  4. Visit the waterfront and see the Detroit river.

Here are some other places you could visit while in Windsor

  • Point Pelee
  • Pelee Island
  • Detroit

If you are a fan of good food and good alcohol, you may want to visit Windsor.
